For (potential) clients

  • A collective can also be called a 'pool', it's a fancy name for a bunch of passionate people helping each other finding work and passing along any work they don't have the time for and knowledge of. Instead of constantly having to approach different people, asking if they are available and have the required knowledge, we make it easier to find exactly what you are looking for!

  • If you want to hire everyone on your own, go ahead, no one is stopping you. Using Cursief helps to create a single point for contacting, we make sure everyone you selected is available at the same time and if necessary we can help finding some else who fits better in your team. Let us do the work!

  • After your members are picked and submitted, we will contact all the members and between them they will decide who is going to be the primary contact for you. We want to prevent that you have to approach everyone seperately, that's something we take care of.

  • We won't be between you and the Cursief member(s), they will send you seperate invoices and you'll sign seperate contract with them.
